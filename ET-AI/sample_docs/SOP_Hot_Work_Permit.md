# SOP-SAF-007 — Hot Work Permit Procedure

Document type: Standard Operating Procedure | Rev 6 | Owner: Safety Department

## Purpose

Establishes the documented permit-to-work procedure for hot work (welding,
cutting, grinding, open flame) in hazardous and non-hazardous areas of
PLANT-01, in line with OISD-105 and OISD-118 requirements.

## Procedure

1. The executing agency raises a Hot Work Permit request identifying the
   exact location, equipment tag, duration, and nature of hot work.
2. The area operator conducts a gas test at the work location. LEL must
   read 0% before the permit is issued. Repeat the gas test every 2 hours.
3. The Safety Officer verifies: fire extinguisher and running water hose
   at site, welding machine earthing, flash-back arrestors on gas cutting
   sets, and removal of combustible material within 15 metres.
4. A trained fire watcher is posted for the full duration of hot work and
   for 30 minutes after completion.
5. The permit is valid for one shift only and must be closed by the area
   operator with a joint site inspection.
6. All permits are filed and retained for 3 years for audit purposes.

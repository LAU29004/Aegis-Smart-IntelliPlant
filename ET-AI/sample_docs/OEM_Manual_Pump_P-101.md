# OEM Manual — Centrifugal Pump Model CP-450X (Tag: P-101)

Manufacturer: FlowServe Industries Ltd. | Model: CP-450X | Serial: FS-2019-88412

## Section 1 — Equipment Overview

The CP-450X is a single-stage centrifugal pump rated for continuous duty in
process water and light hydrocarbon service. Plant tag P-101 serves Cooling
Water Circuit, Line 3.

Rated flow: 450 m3/h | Rated head: 65 m | Motor: 90 kW, 2960 RPM
Design pressure: 16 barg | Design temperature: 80 °C

## Section 2 — Operating Limits

- Minimum suction pressure: 1.5 bar. Operation below this limit causes
  cavitation and dry running of the mechanical seal.
- Maximum casing vibration: 4.5 mm/s RMS measured at bearing housing.
  Above 7.1 mm/s, shut down immediately and investigate.
- Bearing housing temperature: normal 55–70 °C, alarm at 85 °C, trip at 95 °C.
- Suction strainer differential pressure: clean the strainer when DP exceeds
  0.4 bar. A blocked strainer is the leading cause of low suction pressure.

## Section 3 — Mechanical Seal

The pump uses a cartridge mechanical seal, assembly MS-101-B. The seal is
water-lubricated and must never run dry. Dry running for more than 30 seconds
destroys the seal faces. After any low-suction event, inspect the seal for
leakage before returning the pump to service. Always verify suction pressure
is at least 1.5 bar before restart; the recommended value after maintenance is
2.0 bar or higher.

## Section 4 — Maintenance Intervals

### Section 4.3 — Bearing Replacement Interval

Bearings (DE: SKF 6312, NDE: SKF 6310) shall be replaced every 16,000 running
hours or 24 months, whichever is earlier. Under high ambient temperature
(above 40 °C) or repeated start/stop duty, reduce the replacement interval to
8,000 hours. Regreasing interval: every 2,000 hours with lithium-complex
grease, 30 g per bearing. Persistent bearing failures at intervals shorter
than 12 months indicate misalignment, soft foot, or lubrication contamination
and require a root cause investigation, not just replacement.

### Section 4.4 — Preventive Maintenance Schedule

- Monthly: vibration reading, bearing temperature log, seal leak check.
- Quarterly: strainer cleaning, coupling inspection, alignment check.
- Annually: full overhaul inspection, impeller clearance check.

## Section 5 — Troubleshooting Guide

| Symptom | Probable Cause | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Low discharge pressure | Blocked suction strainer, worn impeller | Clean strainer, check impeller clearance |
| High vibration | Bearing wear, misalignment, cavitation | Check bearings, laser alignment |
| Seal leakage | Dry running, worn seal faces | Replace seal assembly MS-101-B, verify suction pressure |
| Bearing overheating | Over-greasing, contamination, overload | Regrease per Section 4.3, check load |

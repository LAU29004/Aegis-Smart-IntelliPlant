# OISD-118 — Layouts for Oil and Gas Installations (Requirements Extract)

Document type: Regulation reference | Indexed for compliance mapping

This extract lists key mandatory requirements from OISD-118 applicable to
PLANT-01 storage and utility areas.

## Requirement 5.2 — Inter-Facility Spacing

Minimum separation distances between storage tanks, process units, and
utility blocks shall be maintained as per Table 3 of OISD-118. The spacing
between any two adjacent Class-A storage tanks shall not be less than the
diameter of the larger tank. Spacing verification shall be documented and
re-verified after any facility modification.

## Requirement 7.4 — Firewater System

The firewater network shall be capable of meeting the design flow rate for
a minimum of 4 hours. A firewater flow test shall be conducted at least
once every 6 months and records of the test flow and pressure readings
shall be maintained and available for audit.

## Requirement 9.1 — Hot Work in Hazardous Areas

Hot work within hazardous areas shall be controlled through a documented
permit-to-work system, including gas testing, fire watch, and time-bound
permit validity as detailed in OISD-105.

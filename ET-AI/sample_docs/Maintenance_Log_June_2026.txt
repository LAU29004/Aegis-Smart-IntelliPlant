Entry 2026-06-14 | Equipment: P-101 (Cooling Water Pump) | WO-2026-0614
Type: Breakdown / Failure
FAILURE REPORT: Pump P-101 tripped on high seal leakage at 02:15 hrs on
14 June 2026. Mechanical seal failure confirmed on strip-down.

Root cause: Dry running of the mechanical seal due to low suction
pressure. Suction strainer was found heavily blocked with debris,
causing suction pressure to fall below the 1.5 bar minimum specified in
the OEM manual. Seal faces showed heat damage consistent with dry running.

Corrective action: Seal assembly MS-101-B replaced with new cartridge
seal. Suction strainer cleaned and flushed. Suction line back-flushed.
Suction pressure verified at 2.1 bar before restart. Strainer DP
monitoring added to daily operator round.

Equipment returned to service on 16 June 2026 after 48-hour observation
period. No leakage observed.
Downtime: 52 hours. Technician: Suresh Patil. Approved by: Rajesh Kumar

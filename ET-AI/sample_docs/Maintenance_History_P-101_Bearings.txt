PLANT-01 — EQUIPMENT FAILURE HISTORY EXTRACT
Equipment: P-101 Cooling Water Pump (Model CP-450X)
Subject: Bearing failure records 2024–2025

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Record 2024-09-05 | WO-2024-0905 | Type: Breakdown / Failure
Drive-end bearing failure on P-101. High vibration (9.8 mm/s) and bearing
housing temperature 92 degC. Bearing SKF 6312 replaced. Grease found
darkened — possible contamination. Pump realigned. Downtime 14 hours.
Technician: Suresh Patil

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Record 2025-04-12 | WO-2025-0412 | Type: Breakdown / Failure
Repeat drive-end bearing failure on P-101, seven months after previous
replacement. Vibration rose over two weeks from 4.2 to 8.5 mm/s. Bearing
replaced. Laser alignment done; slight soft foot corrected. Lubrication
schedule reviewed. Downtime 12 hours.
Technician: Vikram Joshi

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Record 2025-11-20 | WO-2025-1120 | Type: Breakdown / Failure
Third drive-end bearing failure on P-101 within fifteen months. Failure
interval again approximately seven months. Bearing and grease samples
sent for analysis: water ingress detected in grease. Suspected seal
water spray entering the bearing housing. Deflector installed as interim
measure. OEM manual Section 4.3 notes that repeat failures at intervals
below 12 months indicate a systemic cause requiring root cause
investigation. RCA recommended.
Downtime 16 hours. Technician: Suresh Patil. Approved by: Rajesh Kumar
